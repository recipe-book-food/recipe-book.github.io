Spaghetti Carbonara (#1)

  • Time: 15m
  • Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 300g fresh spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 4 eggs (beaten)
  • 100g parmesan cheese

Step 1: Fry the pancetta for 5mins.
Step 2: Mix the eggs and cheese.
Step 3: Cook the pasta in boiling water for 5mins.
Step 4: Mix it all together and sprinkle with more parmesan.

Toasted banana sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • a banana
  • some butter

Step 1: Put the bread into a toaster and toast for just under 2mins.
Step 2: Chop up the banana.
Step 3: Put the chopped-up banana in the sandwich and take a bite!
